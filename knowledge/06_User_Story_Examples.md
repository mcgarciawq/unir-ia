# User Story Examples

## Purpose

The following examples illustrate the organization's preferred style for writing user stories.

These examples demonstrate consistent structure, clear business value, realistic priorities and proportional estimation.

---

# Example 1

Project:
Customer Portal

Role:
Customer

Goal:
Track the status of an order.

Reason:
To know when the purchase will arrive without contacting customer support.

Description:
Customers should be able to view the current order status, estimated delivery date and shipping information from their personal dashboard.

Priority:
High

Story Points:
3

Estimated Effort:
12 hours

---

# General Observations

All examples follow the organization's internal standards.

Each story:

- describes a single business objective;
- clearly identifies the user role;
- explains the business value;
- provides sufficient functional context;
- avoids implementation details;
- uses realistic story points;
- assigns effort proportional to complexity.

Future user stories should maintain the same level of quality and consistency.
